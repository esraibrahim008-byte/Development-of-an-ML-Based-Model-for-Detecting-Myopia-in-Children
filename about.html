Development of an ML-Based Model for Detecting Myopia in Children

The Myopia Detection System is a web-based application that uses machine learning to support the early detection of myopia in children. The system analyzes key clinical and behavioral factors, including age, sports activity hours, television viewing hours, parental history of myopia, and SPHEQ measurements to generate prediction results. This tool aims to promote early screening and timely intervention, particularly in low-resource environments with limited access to specialized eye care services.
